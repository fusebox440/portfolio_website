Project Innovation

WisMe Research Assistant is an innovative AI-powered research tool designed to streamline academic and professional research processes. This collaborative project focuses on creating an intelligent assistant that can help researchers discover, analyze, and synthesize information from multiple sources efficiently.

As a collaborative effort, I contributed to the conceptualization and development of key features that enhance the research workflow. The application integrates advanced AI capabilities to provide contextual research assistance, automated citation management, and intelligent content summarization.

Collaboration Project

This project represents a collaborative effort with fellow developers to create an AI research assistant that enhances productivity and streamlines the research process for academics and professionals.
